How this works ...

Discover current listings, anywhere you are. With one tap on the screen.

Ever see a 'SOLD' sign while walking by, wondering how much the house sold for? We'll show you the most recent sale at an address around you, from the past one year.

We'll also show you all the current listings 'nearby'. This is typically one to two city blocks, so you can easily get there to check it out.

And you can give us a call or send a text if you want to visit any house, anytime!
