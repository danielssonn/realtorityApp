How does this work?

You can specify additional keywords that will be added to your search.



For example, you can search only for houses that contain the word 'pool' in the listing description.



All of your other search settings such as location, price or bedrooms will still be applied.



Keywords are additive, so adding more keywords will further narrow down your search.



Partial keyword match - if your keyword matches a begining of a longer word, it will be applied.



Keywords are not cases sensitive.
